Frequently Asked Questions

How can I adopt a pet?

To adopt a pet, visit our Adopt page, browse through the available pets, and fill out the adoption form. Our team will contact you to guide you through the process.

What are the adoption fees?

Adoption fees vary depending on the type of pet and their medical needs. Fees cover vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and microchipping.

Can I return a pet if it doesn’t work out?

Yes, we understand that sometimes adoptions don’t work out. You can return the pet within 30 days for a full refund. After 30 days, we still accept returns but without a refund.

How do I report a stray animal?

If you find a stray animal, please visit our Report Stray page and fill out the form with details about the animal and its location. Our team will take it from there.

Do you offer pet care advice?

Yes, we provide free pet care advice to all adopters. You can contact us through our Contact page or visit our blog for tips and resources.

What should I do if my pet gets sick?

If your pet shows signs of illness, contact a veterinarian immediately. We also provide a list of recommended vets on our Resources page.
