About PawConnect

PawConnect is a comprehensive platform dedicated to pet adoption and animal welfare. We collaborate with shelters and rescue groups to rehome abandoned and stray animals. Users can browse adoptable pets, report strays in need, or list animals for adoption. With a mission to ensure every animal finds a safe, loving home — we’re here to help.
